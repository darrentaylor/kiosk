The Pavilion

The University of Pennsylvania Health System is building a 1.5 million square foot medical facility, located on the former site of Penn Tower. This facility will share synergies with and support the Penn Medicine renowned researchers, clinicians and faculty, while anchoring Penn Medicine with connections between the medical and university campus.
